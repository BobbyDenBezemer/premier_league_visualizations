Rankings

The visualization on premier league rankings shows an interactive bar chart. Through a drop down menu the user can pick a given year. The chart will then show the rankings of the given year. The bar chart is made using d3.js

Spread of Teams

The visualization on the distribution of premier league teams across the UK will be an interactive map starting from 1992 until 2014. The user can choose to display a particular year by means of a dropdown menu.
